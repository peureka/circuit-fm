Circuit

A members' club with no house.

It moves with you. Tap in at any venue, see who from the club is here tonight, connect if you want to. Access opens with attendance.

How it works

One product. Three gestures.

Every interaction in Circuit starts with a tap. No downloads, no check-ins, no passwords. You tap, you're in.

  1. 01Tap a venue. Block on the wall. You're checked in.Other members can see you're here tonight.
  2. 02Tap a room. Block on the door. You're at this event.The organiser sees you on their guest list.
  3. 03Tap a person. Their Card. You're invited to join.Every member carries a Card. Every Card is an invitation.

Membership

Access opens with attendance.

No annual fee. No application form. Membership is earned by showing up. The more you attend, the earlier you see what's coming.

  • Curated outings. Screenings, supper clubs, gallery visits, movement. Four formats on rotation across London's best venues and members' clubs.
  • See who's here. Tap in at any venue. See which other members are there tonight. Open, closed, or ghost — you control your visibility.
  • Connect. Say hi to someone in the room. One tap. No chat, no DMs, no profiles. The conversation happens in person.
  • Venue access. Four passes per month to events at partner venues, including top members' clubs. Places you can't normally get into.

How to join

Three ways in. No open signup.

Tap a member's Card. Pass The List. Or get on the waitlist. Circuit is invite-only. The room is the point.
